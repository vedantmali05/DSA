Deep Java DSA Learning Syllabus
===============================

1. ARRAYS & SEARCHING/SORTING
   - Linear & Binary Search
   - Bubble, Insertion, Selection Sort
   - Merge Sort & Quick Sort
   - Two Pointers & Sliding Window techniques

2. LINKED LISTS
   - Singly Linked List (internals & manual implementation)
   - Doubly & Circular Linked Lists
   - Floyd's Cycle Detection, Reversal, & Manipulation

3. STACKS & QUEUES
   - Array & Linked List-based Stacks
   - Queue & Circular Queue implementations
   - Deque & Priority Queue concepts

4. TREES & HEAPS
   - Binary Trees & Traversals (DFS, BFS)
   - Binary Search Trees (BST) (Insertion, Deletion, Search)
   - Balanced Trees (AVL / Red-Black Tree concepts)
   - Binary Heaps & Heap Sort

5. GRAPHS
   - Representations (Adjacency Matrix, Adjacency List)
   - Traversals (BFS, DFS)
   - Shortest Path Algorithms (Dijkstra, Bellman-Ford)
   - Minimum Spanning Trees (Prim's, Kruskal's)

6. RECURSION & DYNAMIC PROGRAMMING
   - Backtracking fundamentals
   - Memoization vs. Tabulation
   - Classic DP problems (Knapsack, LCS, LIS)
